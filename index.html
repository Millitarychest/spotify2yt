What does it do?

by MillitarychestNOV 15th 2020

This is programm helps you migrate your favourite collection of songs from Spotify to Youtube. Now why would anyone want to do this? Well apart from being able to party no matter which platform your on, it also enables you to use things like discord bots, which are usually locked behind Spotify premium, to share your sound with the world.

How do I use it?

- The Interface of Spotify2Yt

First press the "fill token" button, this will then prompt you to log in to your Spotify account so we can gather your playlists. If succesful the field titled "Spotify" should be filled out. Now press the "Get Playlist" Button and select the one you wanna migrate from the drop down menu next to it. Last but not least we need to actually create the Youtube-playlist to do this just enter a name for it in the field where you see "MySpotifyPlaylist" and hit "Create". You will be prompted to sign in to your Google account, this is needed so the programm can create and populate your playlist.
